About India

Let me introduce you to India...

India

India | The Republic Of India | Unity in Diversity
India is one of the most beautiful countries in the world , having one of the seven wonders of the world. This coutry attracts most of the tourists in the world..

Popular Destination

  • ** Delhi, the capital of India and one of the oldest cities of the world, is unlike any other city on the planet.
  • ** Mumbai is in all ways a mega-city driven by power, wealth, glamour and fame , also known Dreamcity.
  • ** Jaipur, the capital of Rajasthan is popularly known as the Pink City.

Spiritual Destination

  • ** Lying in the south of Uttar Pradesh, on the banks of the holy River Ganga, breathes Varanas.
  • ** Tirupati, the holy city is located in the southeastern part of Andhra Pradesh, in Chittoor district.
  • ** Haridwar, the gateway to the Gods, is situated on the right bank of the holy Ganga.

Heritage Destination

  • ** Agra is an ancient city on the banks of the River Yamuna. It finds mention in the Mahabharata.
  • ** Ahmedabad, also known as Amdavad, was named after Sultan Ahmed Shah.
  • ** Udaipur was founded in 1553 by Maharana Udai Singh II as the new capital of the Mewar.
